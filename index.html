Definition

A triangle is a polygon with three edges and three vertices. It has three sides and three angles.

Types

  1. Equilateral triangle
  2. Isosceles triangle
  3. Scalene triangle

Types Description

Equilateral Triangle

Three equal sides
Three equal angles, always 60°

Isosceles Triangle

Two equal sides
Two equal angles

Scalene Triangle

No equal sides
No equal angles
